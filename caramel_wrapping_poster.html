Design Justification
Why 2-sided seal?
Faster cycle time, simpler mechanism, equivalent seal quality for biodegradable wrap versus 3-sided configuration.
Why slot-guided fold?
Highest selection score (16/16): compact, repeatable, precise, low complexity — eliminates operator skill dependency entirely.
